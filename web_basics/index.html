Amazing dogs!

This is a quick introduction to the world of dogs. Dogs are amazing creatures that are the best friends ever. If you never had a dog, means you don't know what real love and dedication to people means. So read on... And may be in couple minutes you will find yourself wondering on the adoption site looking for your best buddy for life.

What are they made of?

  • Unconditional love.
  • Tons of energy.
  • Happy tail.
  • Cute puppy eyes that no one can resist to.

Benefits of having a doggy:

  1. Be loved. All the time. No matter who you are. There is always someone waiting for you at home. It is a great feeling. Trust me. You open your door and the happy tail is there!
  2. Be present to the World: Doggy will make you rediscover the world around you. Early morning walks, beautiful sunrises and gorgeous sunsets. You will spend way more time outside! Hear the birds and workout. At least twice a day.
  3. Stop being a lazy couch potato! Give yourself a break. Who said that waking up early is difficult? When you have a happy tail and cute puppy eyes... not so much! You will have way more hours in a day. Start your day with a nice walk with your best fuzzy buddy and get ready for an awesome day. And in the evening, right before you will be going to bed, take a nice refreshing walk and spent some time reflecting on your day. You fuzzy friend will be happy to give you a company, trust me.
